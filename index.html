About Me

I write character-driven fiction which delves deep into the psyches of helpless or hopeless individuals, as well as exploring relationships that have reached a crisis in communication.

Favourite writers: D.H. Lawrence, Ian McEwan, David Lodge, Doris Lessing.

Currently working as a Sales and Marketing Assistant for W.W. Norton Ltd in London.
